Our Manufacturing Capabilities

Shenyang Chuanyuan Weihua Technology Co., Ltd. is equipped with comprehensive manufacturing capabilities including casting, forging, welding, machining, and crushing equipment integration. We are committed to delivering high-quality industrial components and complete solutions for various sectors.

Casting

Established in 2002, with two 3-ton melting furnaces and a 20-ton heat treatment furnace. Annual capacity of 15,000 tons. Produces gray and ductile iron castings from 5kg to 8 tons, equipped with spectrometers and tensile testers to ensure consistent quality.

Forging

Founded in 2001, equipped with a 2000-ton free forging hydraulic press, 4-ton electro-hydraulic hammer, and ring rolling machines. Annual output: 30,000 tons. Main products include gear shafts, ladle trunnions, converter gear rings, and components for wind power and metallurgical industries.

Welding

Established in 1992, covering 25,000 m² with a lifting capacity of up to 50 tons. Equipped with 4000W fiber laser cutters, flame cutting machines, arc welding robots, 1600T CNC press brakes, and straightening machines. Maximum product dimensions: 15,000×6,000×600 mm.

Machining

Founded in 1989 with a 58,000 m² facility and 100-ton lifting capacity. Over 100 CNC machines. Largest CNC gantry milling and boring machine handles 5.5×16×4 m. Equipped with precision measuring systems such as μ-level CMMs and ultrasonic flaw detectors.

Crushing Equipment

Authorized distributor of Hanxi brand crushers and screening equipment. Our modular lines offer efficient installation and flexible configuration. Products are exported to over 50 countries.

Spare Parts

We maintain a large warehouse of wear parts, copper parts, structural components, and standard hydraulic/electrical units to support fast delivery and one-stop procurement.
